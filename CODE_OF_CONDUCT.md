# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in this project to be respectful, constructive, and welcoming to everyone,
regardless of experience, identity, background, or the kind of contribution they make.

## Expected behaviour

- Be kind and professional in issues, pull requests, and reviews.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions, and give actionable feedback.
- Focus disagreement on ideas, evidence, and implementation choices.
- Respect privacy and do not publish personal information, private addresses, credentials, or
  sensitive operational details.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, personal attacks, deliberate disruption, or sharing
private information without permission are not acceptable.

## Reporting

Report a Code of Conduct concern privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Reports will be
reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical. Maintainers may remove comments,
close discussions, or restrict participation when needed to protect the community.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
